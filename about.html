WHO AM I

I'm a daughter, sister, girlfriend, aunt and to some people a good friend I believe. In my (still kind of short) life I already got to experience so many valuable things, got to see plenty of beautiful places and feel countless amazing feelings, all of which I'm greatly thankful for.

All throughout my life, I couldn't find the right box to fit myself in, which has been both my blessing and my curse. Curse, because of this constant desire to try new things to fit to, which is not always possible so then the FOMO kicks in. Blessing, because of all the things I already got to try, whilst finding the right box for me.

While attending high school, I thought the right path for me would be business and economics, so I applied to Avans University of Applied Sciences in Breda, the Netherlands. However, it didn't take long for me to realise that the world of business isn't really the best fit for me, so I decided to make a little change in my plans.

After a deep rethink of my life, I applied to Czech Technical University in Prague for a Computer Science study programme, which is my current study (let's just hope the last one :D). I really think that technology and IT is the way to go for me, although it has it's downsides too. Just as about everything in life.
