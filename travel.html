hobbies > travel

Travelling is my biggest passion. It caries every combination of feelings possible: excitement, frustration, adrenaline, comfort, discomfort and all the others. Come take a look at some of the places I've been to!

Pakoštane, Croatia

This is the annual trip that my boyfriend's family arranges every summer and I am so lucky to have been a part of it for the past couple of years as well. I love the things Croatia has to offer, whether it's the sea, the views, the nightlife or the relax. But despite all of those things, the best part of this yearly trip is the incredible family company I'm always surrounded by.

Phuket, Thailand

In 2024, we got to spend a whole month exploring this wonderful island called Phuket. We took the trip in the rainy season in Thailand and it was the best decision we made. There were very few tourists and it only rained a couple of days out of the whole month. We got to explore the whole island on a motorbike, took a boat trip to the Phi Phi Islands and left the gratest memories. Can't wait to come back!
