hobbies > music

Music has always had a special place in my life, whether it was to listen to it or to make it myself. When I was little, my biggest dream was to be a musician.

My piano journey started when I was 13 years old and somehow managed to save enough money to buy myself my first stage piano. I have never really had a proper piano lesson, I just learned how to play on my own, song by song, on a very amateur level.

In 2022, I had my first and only performance at a school ball dance, where I played the famous piano composition River Flows In You by Yiruma

I bought a ukulele because I loved how such a simple instrument can spread so much joy. I learned how to play a few chords, then a few songs and now I play very occasionaly. Ukulele is a great string instrument to start with, if you'd ever be interested in learning to play one.
