I'm always trying to keep my body and mind moving, that's why there are many hobbies I found in my life, some of which I maintain regularly to this day, and some which are already in my past. Physical activities have always had a reserved space in my heart, from casual running and excercising, through dancing and playing tennis up to snowboarding and ice-skating. Here are some of the hobbies that influenced my life the most.

Dance

Dancing is one of the things I hold really close to my heart, whether it's competitive dancing, performance dancing or only slow dancing with my partner in the kitchen.

Floorball

There's no such feeling as competing in team sports. It's when I started playing floorball that I got to know the power of being a part of a sports group.

Gym

My gym journey started when I was quite young, which I'm very grateful for. It's one of the hobbies I hope I will continue with for as long as I can.
