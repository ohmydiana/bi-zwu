hobbies > gym

The hobby of working out and excercising has come to me at quite a young age, probably when I was 14. There was a sale in our local fitness centre on monthly memberhips, so me and my friends let ourselves be influenced by this marketing move, and going to the gym has become a long-lasting habit.

Before I started excercising, I have only done team sports, such as floorball or dancing. Therefore, going to the gym felt a little silly at first, because it was so individual. However, I quickly accommodated myself into the gym and it became my comfort place. I liked how quickly I started noticing the increase in my mental and physical well-being and hope that I will never get tired of this habit.

At the beginning of 2025, I helped with filming a couple of videos for a brand called KFIT based in Slovakia. They are focused on manufacturing sportswear and other sports accessories. It was a really pleasant experience and I am already looking forward for the next filming session.
