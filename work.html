Ever since I could, I'd always find a part-time job to keep me occupied and to get a perception of what it is like to get a hold of your own, well deserved, money. This timeline represents some of my experiences with part-time jobs.

Waitress

2019 - 2022

Čarovná špajza in Dubnica nad Váhom was a small restaurant, where I found my first job as a waitress. It was a great experience, which taught me a lot about table manners and fine dining.

Lifeguard

2022 - 2025

During the summer seasons I work as a lifeguard at an outdoor swimming pool called Letka in Nová Dubnica. This job comes with a lot of responsibility, but it's also really rewarding to know your job is to help people should they need it.

McDonald's Crew Member

2021 - 2022

Working as a McDonald's Crew Member I realised how challenging it is to work in a very noisy, kitchen environment, but I am altogether grateful for such an experience as well.

English Tutor

2020 - 2025

Tutoring english has strenghten my patience and teaching abilities. I've been tutoring on my own at first, but for the past few years I am one of the lectors in Freedu education centre.

Sports Camp Supervisor

2024

Together with my partner, we supervised and organised a Happy Holiday daily summer sports camp for children. That involved directing various sport activities and providing both safety and fun for all the children attending the camp.
